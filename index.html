    Get to Know Us

    At RT Tech Shop, we provide more than just gadgets—we deliver reliable solutions that keep you connected and productive. From computers, laptops, phones, printers, networking devices, cables, and accessories, to repair services for damaged laptops and gadgets, we are your trusted one-stop shop for all tech needs. Our priority is customer support excellence, ensuring every customer enjoys a smooth and worry-free experience, all while offering affordable prices that fit your budget.

    Convenience at Your Fingertips

    Shopping at RT Tech is easy and stress-free. Whether you prefer cash on delivery, installment options through Home Credit, or digital payments, we’ve got you covered. Plus, our store location in Kapasigan, Pasig City makes it convenient for you to visit us directly for purchases, product inquiries, or gadget repairs.

    Trusted Payment Options

    Your trust and safety come first. That’s why we offer secure and flexible payment options including Cash, GCash, PayMaya, Bank Transfer, and Home Credit. Every transaction is handled with care, giving you peace of mind with every purchase.

    Mission

    At RT Tech, our mission is to make technology accessible, reliable, and affordable for everyone. We aim to provide not only the latest computers, phones, laptops, printers, and accessories, but also the repair services and technical support our customers need to keep their devices performing at their best. Beyond offering products, we are committed to delivering exceptional customer service, ensuring every customer feels supported before, during, and after their purchase. By offering flexible payment methods such as Cash, GCash, PayMaya, Bank Transfer, and Home Credit, we make shopping simple and worry-free. Our goal is to be a partner in every customer’s digital journey—helping individuals, families, and businesses stay connected, productive, and future-ready.

    Vision

    Our vision is to become a trusted leader in technology solutions and services in the Philippines, known for our integrity, reliability, and customer-first approach. We aspire to create a store where technology is not intimidating but accessible and convenient for everyone, regardless of budget. We envision RT Tech as a go-to hub for affordable yet high-quality gadgets and repair services, where every customer can enjoy peace of mind knowing they are backed by a team that values excellence in customer care. In the long run, we aim to expand our reach, continuously improve our offerings, and build lasting relationships with our community—helping empower Filipinos to embrace technology in their homes, schools, and businesses.
